How to SEO

- by shiva gupta

Introduction

SEO stands for search engine optimization. In simple terms, it is a method of your website to increase its visibility for relevant searches. The more visibility your pages have in search results, the more likely you are to garner attention and attract prospective and existing customers to your business. 

Why is SEO important?

SEO helps you inbring traffic to your blog, website or online store. It can help you target the right audience for your business. With the correct use of SEO you can increase your position in the Search Engine Results Page (commonly known as SERPs), with the use of correct keywords. 

What should you know about SEO?

SEO is a great way to bring so much traffic to your site. About 65% of the global population use Google as their search engine. Thus it is always better to optimize your website for Google. An important point to note is that Google is responsible for what would be on the top of the search table.

Keyword research 

There are two steps to achieving the most success with keywords

Keyword Planning

The first step is to find the correct keyword for your business and volume for that keyword. Volume indicates the number of searches performed for the specific keyword. It is first important to narrow down your niche and plan a set of commonly searched terms for the category. The keyword should be relevant to your business. There are many keyword and SEO tools to find volume and relevant keywords, but the most commonly used are Google Trends, Google Analytics, Google Keyword Planner, and Search Console.

Keyword Content Execution

Once you have narrowed down a keyword, it is important to plan the right kind of content around it. Supposing your business is centered around health and coming up is ‘World Health Day’, a globally recognized day by WHO, then it is important to focus on the event. Social media creatives, articles and campaigns centered around the event must begin planning and execution at least a week or two before the event itself. This helps in setting up a strong foundation for SEO. Your website will be crawled and recognized as original content by Google, if done right. By setting up the SEO to target your website weeks before the event, you can ensure that content pandering to the event when released on the day, will result in your website having a very high possibility of ranking on page 1.

Site optimization

Once you have your keyword list, it is time to implement them into your site content. Include the keywords in your blog titles, product descriptions and constantly cater content to the specific keyword to rank high. Remember that excessive use of keywords in your blogs won't do you any good, keep it to minimum. The key lies in ranking high for the keywords that your business caters to, rather than simply ranking high on the SERP. It establishes your authority as a legitimate source for the specific keyword over time, thus weeding out competitors.

One of the important things to note is keyword results differ according to time. Google Trends shows a detailed graph of over 12 years, showing how the keyword performs during certain times of the year. So even if your website dominates the particular keyword, that may not translate into more clicks if your product is a niche. One healthy practice to involve in such a case is to implement trending keywords as part of your website. However, this does not mean clickbaiting to gain views by abusing a specific trending keyword. Healthy SEO practices help in organic growth which can translate into more results for your website..

Google analytics

We can use Google analytics to measure the incoming traffic to our site. You can’t optimize what you can’t measure. Google analytics contains valuable data that can be used to improve the SEO performance of your business and it is free to use. Monitoring your business helps you in keeping track of your business data. This helps you grow your business at a faster pace.

WordPress

Wordpress is a free open source application which allows you to add some amazing plugins that can aid in SEO. Wordpress is a free web building tool which is free to use and it’s comparatively easy to use as compared to other web building tools. It also offers Yoast SEO and a whole host of other plugins that can help with optimizing your website correctly and rank high. Though Yoast SEO is a premium feature, it is well worth the price tag as it will definitely help in boosting your business.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO is a feature that is a fairly advanced SEO technique. WordPress, Shopify, Wix and more allow you to build a website with ease. But, when your business website is built on a custom CMS (Content Management System), it will allow you more freedom for SEO and boost your views and ranking immensely. Technical SEO plays a key aspect, especially for media companies when it comes to increasing visibility. Working with web developers in building a custom website can help in establishing Technical SEO. This will help in establishing your brand as one of the authorities on a particular subject in the eyes of Google.
