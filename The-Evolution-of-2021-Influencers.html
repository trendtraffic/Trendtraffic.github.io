The Evolution of 2021 Influencers

-By Uttam Kumar

The 2020 COVID-19 lockdown was highly prohibitive for many industries this year. But influencers who create content at home used their positions properly to show their audience through increased interest categories such as food, hobbies or fitness. This targeted focus has helped Instagram, and Facebook Influencers get huge benefits in likes and comments. This has been promoted to marketing campaigns by influential people also saw a rise.
The result shows that in 2021, followers will expect their influencers to keep their expectations from their posts and tone of voice.
So let's look into the future, how these will affect the life of a common people. 

WHAT and WHY INFLUENCERS?

Influencers marketing means connecting with the right audience through the right content on the right platform through the right person. In the coming times, these influencers have proved to be game-changers in the market, or we can say that they are happening. Because in today's era, where people are very alert and less trusting of companies and brands, Influencers can play a big role in maintaining those trusts.
And perhaps this is the only thing that big brands are promoting influencer marketing. 

HIKE of ALTERNATIVE REVENUE STREAMS

When the economy collapsed due to lockdown, many influencers suffered, and the influencers' dependence on the contracts of the brand increased. Many shifted their focus to alternative revenue streams and altered income sources. Many brands rely on affiliate programs and links that provide influencers with opportunities. Big influential people have increased the sales of their wares. More diversified revenue streams mean influential people keep are not dependent on brand deals and maintain tough negotiations.

POWER of MICRO-INFLUENCERS

They have smaller reach figures but higher engagement rates than their macro-level counterparts of more than 40%. They are less costly- and they are very reliable. Micro-effectors are believed to provide great value to brands to tap into very defined micro-communities.
But brands are now targeting influencers who have creativity in their fields. Brands also want that the content present will be more attractive and promote viewerships towards their brand. 

STATISTICS

According to the Influencer Marketing Hub survey, Influencer marketing can be more effective and attractive if done properly. The survey shows that about 2/3 businesses are marketing their brands through influencers, increasing by 17% in 2021.
Mediakix released its Annual Survey Report in 2019. He found that 61% had to face the difficulty of finding good influencers to run their campaigns.
Data from 2014-2019, Google Trends shows that influencers marketing is reducing print advertising.
According to the Influencer Marketing Survey, followed by Bloglovin' survey, 79% considered Instagram the best platform for their campaign. Facebook came second at 46%, followed by YouTube at 36%.
There are around 2 billion YouTubers globally, and 6 in 10 YouTube subscribers will follow advice on what to buy from their favourite producer on their favourite TV or movie personality.

By combining trust and satisfaction, social commerce posts from influencers will become an important part of consumer shopping in 2021.
