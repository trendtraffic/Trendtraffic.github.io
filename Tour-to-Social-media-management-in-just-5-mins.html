Life is just a click away 

Social media is a platform that allows users to have conversations, share information and create web contents. It is a network that connects people from different countries, people with a similar mindset, and nowadays, it's used for marketing various products or services. Social media has become an important element in today's lifestyle.

Social media platforms  

  • Instagram: - According to their convenience, Instagram has become one of the top social networking sites where people share personal information, pictures, videos, blogs, etc. Users create their account while some are private, and some are public. It is a home for many influencers or marketing accounts. Today almost 800 million and above people are accessing it.
  • Facebook :- Facebook is the second top social networking site where users create individual accounts using their email id. Users post pictures, write info on their wall and share pictures or videos. People send friend requests to each other and so are added to each other's friend list. Facebook provides users to create events online. It also notifies people of some special events. 
  • Youtube :- Youtube is a platform where people share online videos and create channels. A viewer can subscribe to multiple channels and view any content uploaded by the creators. A video gets trending when it has the most number of views. A video posted can be of any type preferable. Youtube is used by millions or billions of people worldwide. 
  • Twitter:- Twitter is a platform where people share their thoughts with people on Twitter. It allows users to get their perspective out and write it down in 280 characters. The posts or write-ups on Twitter are called tweets. An individual writes their tweet or retweets other people's tweet.

There is still more to social media applications.

Smart ways to manage social media 

While it comes to access social media it is just a click away, but managing is quite a task. So here are some simple ways for you.

  1. Pre-plan things: When you pre-plan your content, it is easy in many ways. Plan the perfect timings according to your followers. Share your ideas personal thoughts.
  2. Engagement: Post things according to your audience if your audience loves what you post, your content will be trending, Talk to your followers frequently, know their requirements and post.
  3. Followup: Research your profile and get what your audience viewed the most and post according. Grab their attention.
  4. Communication: Communication is the key. Communicate to your audience, reply to them back as you can.
  5. Diversity: Remember not to be boring people love changes. Change your content accordingly so that more people get engaged to you.

Nowadays, many companies use social media to expand their business, which leads to online marketing. Companies gain followers and sell their products online. It is beneficial for sellers and customers. So there we can see social media has taken a step forward in growing the economy. It contributes 40% to world income.
